1. What BannerMod Adds

BannerMod turns multiplayer Minecraft into a feudal strategy sandbox. It does not replace normal survival; it gives your base a political owner, workforce, economy, army, and war objectives.

The three foundations

ConceptMeaningWhere
ClaimProtected Overworld land. Claims control block protection and settlement ownership.Map M
SettlementA living base: fort, homes, workers, citizens, storage, markets, projects, logistics.Surveyor + Wand
StateYour political side: leaders, co-leaders, wars, allies, capitals, government form.War Room U

Default controls

M opens the world map. U opens the War Room. R opens recruit command controls. X opens worker command controls.

Claims are Overworld-only. Nether and End chunks at matching X/Z coordinates are not protected by Overworld claims.

Коротко по-русски

Клейм - защищенная земля в обычном мире. Поселение - живая база с фортом, домами, складами, рынком, рабочими и жителями. Государство - твоя политическая сторона для войн, союзов и управления.

Клавиши: M карта, U War Room, R команды рекрутов, X команды рабочих.

2. Founding A Settlement

  1. Pick a base location and claim a nearby Overworld chunk on the map.
  2. Create a state: /bannermod state create <name>. You become leader.
  3. Craft the two settlement tools.
  4. Build a Starter Fort. This is the keystone; no settlement appears without it.
  5. Use the surveyor to mark anchor and zones. Validate by right-clicking air.
  6. On success, BannerMod creates or binds a claim, creates a SettlementRecord, spawns starter workers, and spawns four free citizens for vacancies.

Tool recipes

Settlement Surveyor Tool: 2 sticks, 2 oak planks, 1 iron ingot in a stairs-shaped pattern.

Building Placement Wand: one gold block above one stick.

If founding fails, read the chat reason: missing state, missing anchor, missing required zone, invalid fort, town too close, no owned claim, or server-side claim rejection.

What to do after bootstrap

  1. Open the map and confirm the claim exists and belongs to your side.
  2. Register storage first, because many later systems use stockpile visibility.
  3. Register market second if you want state promotion and trade hints.
  4. Add homes before growing population too far beyond capacity.
  5. Create farm, mine, lumber camp, and architect workshop to unlock useful vacancies.

Порядок по-русски: клейм -> государство -> surveyor/wand -> Starter Fort -> якорь и зоны -> ПКМ в воздухе -> склад -> рынок -> дома -> рабочие здания.

3. Surveyor And Wand

Settlement Surveyor Tool

  • Right-click the anchor block first. For a fort, anchor should sit inside the authority area.
  • Right-click corners to mark zones.
  • Shift + right-click in air cycles surveyor mode: starter fort, house, farm, mine, lumber camp, smithy, storage, architect workshop, inspect.
  • Shift + right-click on a block cycles zone role: AUTHORITY_POINT, INTERIOR, EXTERIOR, WORK_ZONE, STORAGE, and other roles.
  • Right-click air without shift validates the current session.

Building Placement Wand

  • PLACE: place a prefab/build plan.
  • VALIDATE: verify a manually built structure.
  • REGISTER: register the building or work area with the settlement.

Build Area screens show scan/build status and reject invalid names, empty scans, oversize structures, missing NBT, or bad bounds with visible reasons.

Common validation mistakes

  • Anchor was not set before corners.
  • Wrong surveyor mode was selected.
  • Starter fort has no AUTHORITY_POINT or no INTERIOR.
  • Trying to validate non-fort buildings before the settlement exists.
  • Registered area is outside the friendly claim or belongs to the wrong side.

Важно: Shift+ПКМ в воздухе меняет режим инструмента, Shift+ПКМ по блоку меняет роль зоны. Обычный ПКМ по воздуху запускает проверку.

4. Buildings And What They Do

Starter Fort / Town HallFounding anchor and promotion requirement.Authority point; required for state promotion.
HouseValidated home capacity.Raises resident capacity and home assignment inputs.
StorageWhere resources live; routes can be configured.Stockpile capacity, route hints, logistics signals.

Manual validated buildings and prefab-backed work areas both feed settlement snapshots. Overlapping live work areas are deduped to avoid double-counting.

Recommended build order

  1. Starter Fort: unlock settlement.
  2. Storage: give the settlement a visible stockpile.
  3. Farm: make the first food economy legible.
  4. Market: unlock trade visibility and state-promotion requirement.
  5. Homes: increase resident capacity and reduce housing pressure.
  6. Mine/lumber/architect: unlock material and construction loops.

Постройки: форт запускает поселение, склад дает запасы, рынок нужен для торговли и повышения, дома дают вместимость, ферма/шахта/лес дают вакансии.

5. Settlement Life Cycle

  1. Bootstrap: starter fort validation creates the settlement and first population.
  2. Vacancies: farms, mines, lumber camps, architect workshops, and barracks-like prefab roles create jobs.
  3. Citizens convert: a free citizen near a vacancy can bind to its anchor and become the required worker or recruit.
  4. Work orders: settlements publish tasks such as hauling and fetching resources. Workers claim, complete, cancel, or release them.
  5. Growth and projects: stockpile, market, resident capacity, shortages, and governor signals create project hints.
  6. Politics: once fort/town hall, storage, and market exist, the settlement can promote its state to STATE.
Four free citizens appear from normal starter-fort bootstrap. They are not the same as starter workers; they are the survival source for vacancy professions.

What the governor is telling you

  • Shortage blockers mean jobs exist but resources are missing.
  • Project hints are not magic quests; they are derived from capacity, goods, markets, and governance.
  • Incidents warn about supply, tax pressure, and settlement health.
  • Strategic labels tell why this settlement matters politically.

Цикл: жители занимают вакансии, рабочие выполняют заказы, склады и рынки дают снабжение, губернатор показывает проблемы и подсказки развития.

6. Economy And Logistics

The governor logistics panel is the best in-game ledger. It shows pending/claimed/completed work orders, shortages, stockpile capacity, missing goods, project hints, strategic role, route cost, specialties, war objectives, and loyalty pressure.

Storage routes

Storage Area route fields use Apply route. The screen validates destination UUID, item filters, amount, and priority before sending. Valid entries show destination or blocked reason.

On long land routes, eligible merchants/couriers may automatically ride a nearby unoccupied horse or other server-approved mount. If no mount is available, they keep hauling on foot; route clear/failure makes them dismount.

Strategic roles

  • surplus hub farms plus storage.
  • junction market market plus authored routes.
  • chokepoint fort fort plus route storage.
  • water gate port or water access.

Water/ports are cheap supply. Multiple routes are reliable. One route is vulnerable. Landlocked settlements are expensive and may specialize in preserved food or worked materials.

How to read logistics in play

  • If storage exists but goods are missing, create source buildings or routes.
  • If a settlement is landlocked, build local production instead of relying on distant hauling.
  • If a route is single and important, defend it: it is a future war objective.
  • If a port exists, use it as a strategic gate, not just a warehouse.

Экономика: склад показывает ресурсы, рынок связывает торговлю, вода удешевляет снабжение, один маршрут уязвим, landlocked-поселение должно специализироваться.

7. Workers And Citizens

Workers

Workers operate registered work areas and settlement work orders. They must be inside a friendly claim, belong to the right side, and see a valid registered work area.

  • Right-click a worker to open inventory and profession/task controls.
  • Use X for worker group commands: follow, guard, move to position, stop.
  • If a claim is captured, deleted, or mismatched, work may stop.

Citizens

Citizens are unassigned population. They can occupy vacancies and convert into workers or recruits. If no citizen is available, add housing, keep the settlement safe, and check whether the building actually registered a vacancy.

Worker failure checklist

  1. Is the work area inside a friendly claim?
  2. Does the worker belong to your side?
  3. Was the building validated or registered?
  4. Does the building type create the profession you expect?
  5. Does the settlement have storage/resource access for that job?

Рабочие: ПКМ открывает инвентарь, X дает групповые команды. Если стоят без дела - проверь клейм, сторону, регистрацию здания и ресурсы.

8. Recruits And Command

Recruits follow owners, consume upkeep, and can be issued individual or group orders. Use R near recruits or from command UI.

Orders

  • Hold
  • Follow
  • Regroup
  • Wander
  • Come to me
  • Patrol
  • Move to position
  • Face
  • Attack
  • Ranged fire
  • Aggression
  • Mount / siege-machine crew

Stances

StanceBehavior
LOOSEFlexible movement; shield blocks about 45%.
LINE_HOLDHolds line harder; shield blocks about 55%.
SHIELD_WALLSlow frontal wall; shield absorbs about 70% inside a 120 degree front cone.

Pikes brace against cavalry, reach weapons strike farther, second-rank spearmen can poke through allies, side hits do x1.15, back hits do x1.5.

Practical command doctrine

  • Use LINE_HOLD for stable fronts and to keep ranged troops from drifting forward.
  • Use SHIELD_WALL when arrows or frontal melee pressure matter more than speed.
  • Face commands matter: shields protect mostly forward.
  • Queue or selection-aware command paths avoid accidentally moving the whole army.
  • Mount/siege-machine orders now go through the same command intent pipeline as army orders.

Рекруты: R команды, строй и стойка решают бой. Щиты работают спереди, пики против кавалерии, удары в бок/спину сильнее.

9. States And Government

States are controlled in the War Room or with commands.

  • /bannermod state create <name> creates a state.
  • /bannermod state list lists states.
  • /bannermod state info <id-fragment> shows details.
  • /bannermod state setcapital <entity> [pos] sets capital.
  • /bannermod state status <entity> <status> changes status.

Government form

Monarchy keeps key authority leader-only. Republic lets co-leaders share authority for status, capital, color, charter, claim edits, ally invites, siege placement, and legal war outcomes.

Promotion

To become a full STATE, the settlement must have starter fort or town hall, storage, and market. The War Room promotion button sends a server-checked request and reports missing infrastructure.

Authority rules

  • Leader can perform core political actions.
  • Co-leaders gain shared authority in republics.
  • Operators may perform admin-only outcomes where marked.
  • Claim editing by political authority follows the same leader/co-leader model.

Политика: монархия - решения у лидера. Республика - со-лидеры получают общую власть. STATE требует форт/ратушу, склад и рынок.

10. War Room And Wars

Open the War Room with U. It shows battle-window banner, active wars, details, allies, target positions, and siege standards.

Main buttons

  • States: state list/detail/actions.
  • Declare war: choose attacker, defender, goal, casus belli.
  • Cancel / Occupy / Annex: server-authoritative outcomes.
  • Allies: invite, accept, decline, or cancel ally invites before war activation.
  • Place siege here: plants a standard after server validation.

War commands

/bannermod war declare, list, info, cancel, whitepeace, tribute, vassalize, demilitarize, occupations, and revolts mirror UI/admin flows.

Battle windows

The top banner tells whether battle is open or closed and when it changes. PvP war damage requires active/in-siege war state, open battle window, and registered participants.

Allies

Allies are consent-based before war activation: invite, accept, decline, or cancel. Peaceful entities cannot join attackers.

Война: War Room показывает фазы, стороны, цели, союзников и кнопки итогов. Союзы требуют согласия. Некоторые исходы доступны только операторам.

11. Sieges And Standards

Siege Standard recipe

GNGDBDGSG

G gold block, N emerald block, D diamond, B any banner, S stick. Stack size 16.

How to place one

  1. Have an active war.
  2. Open War Room, select war, stand at the target place.
  3. Click Place siege here, or use /bannermod siege place <warId> <side> [pos] [radius].

Default radius is 64 blocks. The block entity syncs war and side to clients; renderer colors the cap by political color. Standing inside an active siege zone shows a HUD banner.

What standards do

  • They define battle/siege objective space.
  • Friendly recruits escort same-side standards.
  • Enemy recruits can target and damage standards.
  • Logistics objectives like stockpiles, route junctions, water gates, and surplus stores tell players what matters strategically.

Siege commands and counters

/bannermod siege list [warId] lists standards. /bannermod siege remove <standardId> is op-only. A standard's control pool drops when valid attackers damage it; reaching zero destroys it.

Place standards where defenders must respond: near stockpiles, routes, crossings, ports, gates, or surplus stores. Do not place them randomly; they are political pressure tools.

Осада: стандарт задает радиус цели. Свои рекруты охраняют, чужие атакуют. Ставь стандарт у склада, маршрута, порта или прохода - там, где потеря реально больна.

12. Denials And Troubleshooting

  • Claim edit denied: check Overworld, distance, overlap, max size, currency, server config, and authority.
  • Settlement will not found: create/join state, validate starter fort, mark anchor and required zones, avoid too-close same-nation towns.
  • Workers idle: check friendly claim, ownership, registered work area, matching vacancy, and available resources.
  • No citizens take jobs: check free citizen availability and distance to vacancy anchor.
  • Cannot promote state: register fort/town hall, storage, and market.
  • War action locked: check leadership, war phase, peaceful status, cooldowns, and whether the outcome is op-only.
  • Siege placement denied: check live war, side leadership, target position, and server validation.
Rule of thumb: if a UI action changes gameplay, the server is the authority. Client screens preview and request; accepted state comes back through sync or chat feedback.

This almanac is maintained from MULTIPLAYER_GUIDE_EN.md, MULTIPLAYER_GUIDE_RU.md, docs/STATUS.md, and live code contracts.

Если что-то не работает: проверь мир, дистанцию, власть, клейм, регистрацию здания, ресурсы, фазу войны и серверный текст отказа. Клиент просит, сервер решает.

13. Code Tables: Settlement

Surveyor modes

BOOTSTRAP_FORT, HOUSE, FARM, MINE, LUMBER_CAMP, SMITHY, STORAGE, ARCHITECT_BUILDER, INSPECT_EXISTING.

Zone roles

AUTHORITY_POINT, INTERIOR, SLEEPING, WORK_ZONE, FORT_PERIMETER, ENTRANCE, STORAGE, PREFAB_FOOTPRINT.

Building records

  1. Surveyor validates a structure into a building snapshot.
  2. Valid manual structures become ValidatedBuildingRecord.
  3. Settlement refresh merges valid records and live work areas.
  4. The merged snapshot calculates capacity, jobs, stockpiles, markets, desired goods, projects, supply signals, and promotion readiness.

Таблица поселения: эти значения не лор, а реальные серверные enum-ы. Если режим или зона выбраны неверно, серверная проверка не даст нужного здания.

17. Русский быстрый маршрут

  1. Открой карту M, заклейми чанк в обычном мире.
  2. Создай государство: /bannermod state create Название.
  3. Скрафти Surveyor Tool и Building Placement Wand.
  4. Построй Starter Fort, отметь якорь и зоны AUTHORITY_POINT + INTERIOR.
  5. ПКМ в воздухе валидирует. Успех дает SettlementRecord, рабочих и четыре свободных жителя.
  6. Зарегистрируй склад, ферму, рынок, дома, шахту/лес/мастерскую.
  7. Открой губернатора: смотри дефициты, проекты, роли, маршруты и pressure.
  8. Открой War Room U: управляй государством, войнами и осадами.

18. Русский бой и осады

Рекруты командуются через R. Стойки: LOOSE, LINE_HOLD, SHIELD_WALL. Щиты лучше работают лицом к врагу; пики держат кавалерию; удары сбоку и сзади сильнее.

Осада начинается не с драки, а с цели. Скрафти Siege Standard, выбери войну в War Room, встань у важного места и нажми Place siege here. Радиус по умолчанию 64 блока.

Ставь знамена у склада, маршрута, моста, порта, рынка или chokepoint fort. Свои рекруты охраняют знамена, чужие атакуют и сбивают control pool. Игроки могут наносить war PvP-урон только при правильной фазе войны, открытом battle window и участии обеих сторон.
